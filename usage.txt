HOW TO USE
==========

1. Run the script:
   ./recon.sh -d example.com -w my_wordlist.txt

FOLDER STRUCTURE GENERATED:
recon_example.com_YYYYMMDD/
├── passive/
│   └── passive-subdomain.txt (Subfinder results)
├── active/
│   ├── resolved.txt          (Resolved passive subdomains)
│   ├── bruteforce-results.txt (Puredns brute results)
│   ├── permutations.txt      (Alterx + Puredns results)
│   └── final-resolved.txt    (Combined unique resolved list)
├── web/
│   └── metadata.txt          (Httpx technology & status codes)
├── network/
│   ├── services.txt          (Naabu port scan results)
│   └── nmap_report.txt       (Detailed Nmap scan of the domain)
└── reports/
    ├── final_report.json     (Clean JSON data)
    └── final_report.txt      (Human-readable summary)

Note: No -r (resolvers) flag is needed; Puredns uses system defaults.
